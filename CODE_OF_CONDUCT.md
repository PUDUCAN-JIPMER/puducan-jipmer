# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Giving constructive feedback
- Helping new contributors

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information

## Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove or reject contributions that violate this code.
